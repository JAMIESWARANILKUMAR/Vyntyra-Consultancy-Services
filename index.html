Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What services does Vyntyra provide?

We offer a wide range of IT consultancy services including software development, web development, app development, data security, digital marketing, and corporate training solutions.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@vyntyra.com or call us at +91 93905 15106. We are available Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 6 PM IST.

Do you offer custom software solutions?

Yes, we specialize in building custom software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of your business, from simple websites to complex enterprise applications.

Where is Vyntyra located?

Our headquarters are located in Unit no 601, 5th Floor, Grand Palace, Plot no 238 & 239, Dwaraka Nagar, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh 530016, India.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-cost projects for well-defined scopes and time-and-material models for evolving projects. We also have specific packages for web development and digital marketing.

Do you provide maintenance and support after deployment?

Absolutely. We provide free deployments for 1 year with Advanced BOT Protection, along with day-to-day verification and bug fixing to ensure your application runs smoothly.

How long does it take to build a website or app?

The timeline varies based on complexity. A basic website can take 1 week, while a custom mobile app or complex software solution may take 1-3 months or more. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Can you help with digital marketing and SEO?

Yes, we have a dedicated digital marketing team that provides SEO, social media marketing, content strategy, and paid advertising services to help grow your online presence.

Do you work with startups?

Yes, we love working with startups! We offer scalable solutions and special packages designed to help startups launch their MVP (Minimum Viable Product) quickly and cost-effectively.

Is my data secure with Vyntyra?

Security is our top priority. We implement industry-standard security protocols, including data encryption, secure coding practices, and regular security audits to protect your sensitive information.
